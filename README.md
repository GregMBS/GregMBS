### **Gregory M. Blumenthal, PhD**
**Principal, GMBS Consulting**
*Data Science | Strategic Analytics | Complex Systems Modeling*

Dr. Gregory Blumenthal is a data scientist and strategic consultant who specializes in translating complex, high-dimensional data into actionable intelligence. As the Principal of **GMBS Consulting**, he helps organizations bridge the gap between raw technical analytics and human-centric decision-making, serving clients ranging from international cybersecurity firms to local social advocacy groups.

With a career grounded in the rigorous modeling of complex biological systems, Dr. Blumenthal brings a unique scientific discipline to business intelligence. His background includes extensive work in **toxicology and risk analysis**, where he developed objective criteria for PBPK (Physiologically Based Pharmacokinetic) models for the **US EPA** and contributed to projects for the **NIH, VA, and NASA** (receiving a NASA award in 2014).

Leveraging this expertise in predictive modeling, Dr. Blumenthal now focuses on data-driven strategy, secure web application deployment, and social impact analytics. His current work includes:
*   **Strategic Development:** Serving as a key advisor for platforms like *remoting.work* and *Security Tapestry*, where he supports international teams across Europe and Africa.
*   **Social Data Science:** Co-authoring the annual **Memphis Poverty Fact Sheet**, a critical resource that uses demographic data to drive policy and community understanding of poverty dynamics.
*   **Technical Leadership:** delivering full-stack solutions (Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP/Python/R) that prioritize ethical data use, bias mitigation, and contextual accuracy.

Dr. Blumenthal holds a **B.A. in Chemistry from Vanderbilt University** and a **PhD** with a research focus on toxicology and risk assessment. He is based in Memphis, TN, where he combines his "optimize SQL queries in my head" technical capability with a passion for making real sense of data for the betterment of business and society.
